# Pork Carnitas

## Ingredients
- 4 lbs Pork Butt
- 3-4 Tablespoons of salt
- 1 Teaspoon of pepper
- 1 Tablespoon of ground cumin
- 1 Tablespoon of Oregano
- 1 large Onion
- 8 Cloves of Garlic, smashed
- 2 Limes, juiced
- 2 Large Oranges, or 3/4 Cup of Orange Juice
- 3/4 Cup of Coke
- 2 Bay Leaves




## Steps
- Cook for 10 hours
